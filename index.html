About Me

Mahmoud Sayed Youssef Kotb

Mahmoud Sayed Youssef Kotb is an AI engineer and educator with a Bachelor's degree in Bioinformatics from Assiut University. He built a solid foundation in mathematics, programming, and machine learning, mastering tools like Python, C++, C#, R, and TensorFlow. Mahmoud has taught over 10,000 students through Arabic-language courses on Udemy and YouTube, covering Discrete Mathematics, Probability, Data Structures, and Machine Learning. His content has gained over 500,000 views and strong positive feedback for clarity and depth. He built and deployed intelligent systems in real-world settings. His graduation project Vitalism Solution, a real-time video-based system for estimating vital signs, was selected as a finalist in ISEIC-2023 and a semi-finalist in Microsoft Imagine Cup 2023. Mahmoud also served in Egypt’s National Company for Roads, programming high-scale systems used by millions of drivers across 70% of Egypt’s toll roads. He has strong experience in AI, deep learning, back-end development, and signal processing, and is planning to pursue an MSc in Artificial Intelligence in 2025. Mahmoud combines technical depth, teaching excellence, and a passion for community impact through his work and content.
